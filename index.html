FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Is this for sim or real track use?

Both. The workflow is built around sessions, lap comparisons, setup learning, and debriefs. Regardless of where the data comes from.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, the plan is mobile-first capture (Just click record) with analysis and debrief workflows that stay fast and simple.

Do I need a MoTeC / AIM system?

No. Those are great, but Apex is designed to support multiple sources over time and keep the workflow consistent (capture, compare, debrief, learn). We support importing data from most major data aquisition systems. It's not required, but more data will only make the analysis and suggestions better.

How do setup suggestions work?

They connect symptoms (understeer/oversteer phases, entry/exit issues) to patterns in the data and your notes, then track what you changed and what improved.

How do debriefs work?

Debriefs summarize the biggest opportunities: braking points, apex speeds, throttle application, and consistency, turning data into action items and a practice plan.

Is my data private?

That’s the goal: privacy-first by default, with explicit sharing/export when you choose.
